## ?? GETTING STARTED

### Step 1: Account Setup (5 minutes)
1. Check your email for login credentials
2. Visit: https://lessonsparkusa.com
3. Sign in with your credentials
4. Complete your profile setup

### Step 2: First Lesson (15-20 minutes)
1. Click "Create New Lesson" on dashboard
2. Fill in your lesson details:
   - Scripture passage
   - Age group
   - Teaching style preferences
   - Theological perspective
3. Click "Generate Lesson"
4. Wait ~76 seconds for your lesson
5. Review and download (PDF or DOCX)

### Step 3: Join Facebook Group (5 minutes)
1. Check your email for Facebook group invitation
2. Join: "LessonSparkUSA Beta Testers" (Private Group)
3. Introduce yourself to fellow testers
4. Ask questions and share experiences

## ?? TESTING TIPS

### What to Test
- **Lesson Quality:** Is the content theologically sound?
- **Customization:** Do your preferences show up in the output?
- **Age Appropriateness:** Does it match your selected age group?
- **Usability:** Is the interface easy to use?
- **Export Features:** Do PDF/DOCX exports work well?

### Different Scenarios to Try
1. **Different Age Groups:** Try Children, Youth, and Adult
2. **Various Scripture Types:** Narrative, Teaching, Poetry, Prophecy
3. **Different Lengths:** Short passages vs. longer chapters
4. **Theology Profiles:** Test your church's theological perspective
5. **Languages:** Try English, Spanish, or French if applicable

## ?? REPORTING BUGS

### Bug Report Checklist
When reporting bugs, include:

1. **What happened:** Brief description
2. **What you expected:** What should have happened
3. **Steps to reproduce:** 
   - Step 1: I did this
   - Step 2: Then I did that
   - Step 3: Then this happened
4. **Screenshots:** If visual issue
5. **Browser:** Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge
6. **Device:** Desktop, tablet, mobile

### Example Bug Report
```
BUG: Lesson generation times out for long passages

What happened: Got a timeout error after 2 minutes
What I expected: Lesson should generate in ~76 seconds
Steps to reproduce:
1. Selected Romans chapter 8 (entire chapter)
2. Chose Adult age group
3. Clicked Generate Lesson
4. Waited 2+ minutes, got error
Browser: Chrome on Windows 11
Screenshot: [attached]
```

## ? FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

### Q: How long does lesson generation take?
**A:** Approximately 76 seconds. If it takes longer, that's important feedback!

### Q: Can I edit the generated lessons?
**A:** Absolutely! Lessons are starting points. Edit in Word or Google Docs after export.

### Q: What if I find a theological error?
**A:** This is CRITICAL feedback! Report immediately via in-app feedback or email Lynn directly.

### Q: Can I share lessons with others?
**A:** During beta, please keep lessons confidential. After launch, share freely!

### Q: What happens after the beta period?
**A:** You'll have lifetime free access as a thank you for your participation.

### Q: I'm not very tech-savvy. Will I struggle?
**A:** We designed this for volunteer teachers, not tech experts. Your feedback on usability is invaluable!

### Q: Can I test in Spanish?
**A:** Yes! The platform supports English, Spanish, and French.
