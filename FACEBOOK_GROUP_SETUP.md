# FACEBOOK GROUP SETUP GUIDE
## BibleBibleLessonSpark Beta Testers Private Group

### Purpose
Create a private Facebook group for beta testers to:
- Share experiences and feedback
- Ask questions and get quick answers
- Build community among Baptist Bible teachers
- Provide real-time support during beta testing

---

## ?? STEP-BY-STEP SETUP

### Step 1: Create the Group (5 minutes)

1. **Go to Facebook**
   - Visit: https://www.facebook.com/groups/create
   - Make sure you're logged into your personal Facebook account

2. **Group Settings**
   - **Name:** BibleBibleLessonSpark Beta Testers
   - **Privacy:** Private (only members can see posts)
   - **Visibility:** Hidden (people need invite link to find it)

3. **Description**
```
   Welcome to the BibleBibleLessonSpark Beta Testing Community!
   
   This private group is for beta testers helping shape the future of Baptist Bible study lesson creation. 
   
   ?? What We Do:
   - Test and provide feedback on BibleBibleLessonSpark
   - Share experiences and tips
   - Support fellow Bible teachers
   - Influence platform development
   
   ?? Beta Period: 3-4 weeks
   ?? Limited to 20 testers
   
   Questions? Contact Lynn Eckeberger
   ?? lynn@bibleBibleLessonSpark.com
   ?? 214.893.5179
```

4. **Group Type:** Study group or Professional networking

5. **Click "Create"**

---

### Step 2: Configure Group Settings (10 minutes)

1. **Go to Group Settings** (Admin Tools > Settings)

2. **Privacy Settings**
   - [x] Hide group from search
   - [x] Require admin approval for new members
   - [x] Only admins can approve posts (for first week, then open it up)

3. **Membership Approval**
   - Set up 3 membership questions:
   
   **Question 1:** What is your full name?
   **Question 2:** What church do you serve?
   **Question 3:** Did you receive a beta invitation from Lynn Eckeberger?

4. **Group Rules** (click "Add Rule")
```
   1. Be Respectful
      We're all servants in ministry. Treat everyone with Christian love and respect.
   
   2. Stay On Topic
      This group is for BibleBibleLessonSpark beta testing. Other discussions belong elsewhere.
   
   3. Constructive Feedback Only
      Honest criticism is welcome. Unproductive complaining is not.
   
   4. Protect Privacy
      Don't share personal information of others without permission.
   
   5. No Self-Promotion
      This isn't a place to advertise your church, books, or services.
```

5. **Group Tags** (for organizing posts)
   - Bug Report
   - Feature Request
   - Success Story
   - Question
   - Announcement

## ?? MODERATION TIPS

### Daily Tasks (5-10 minutes/day)
- [ ] Approve new member requests
- [ ] Respond to questions
- [ ] Acknowledge bug reports
- [ ] Thank people for feedback
- [ ] Address any conflicts

### Weekly Tasks (30 minutes/week)
- [ ] Post weekly check-in
- [ ] Summarize feedback trends
- [ ] Celebrate wins
- [ ] Update pinned posts if needed

### What to Watch For
- ? Active participation (good!)
- ? Helpful discussions (good!)
- ?? Off-topic posts (redirect kindly)
- ?? Negativity without solutions (encourage constructive feedback)
- ? Disrespectful behavior (address immediately)

## ?? SUCCESS METRICS

**Healthy Group Indicators:**
- 80%+ of beta testers join

- Questions answered within 24 hours
- Mix of bug reports, questions, and positive feedback
- Members helping each other
