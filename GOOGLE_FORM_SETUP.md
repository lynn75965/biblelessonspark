# GOOGLE FORM SETUP FOR BETA TESTING

## Step 1: Create the Form
1. Go to: https://docs.google.com/forms
2. Make sure you're logged into: bibleBibleLessonSpark@gmail.com
3. Click "Blank" to create new form
4. Title: "BibleBibleLessonSpark Beta Program Sign-Up"

## Step 2: Add Questions (in this exact order)

**Question 1: Name**
- Type: Short answer
- Required: Yes
- Label: "Full Name"

**Question 2: Email**
- Type: Short answer
- Required: Yes
- Label: "Email Address"
- Validation: Text > Email

**Question 3: Church Name**
- Type: Short answer
- Required: Yes
- Label: "Church Name & Location"

**Question 4: Teaching Experience**
- Type: Paragraph
- Required: Yes
- Label: "How long have you been teaching Bible studies? What age groups?"

**Question 5: Current Age Group**
- Type: Multiple choice
- Required: Yes
- Label: "Which age group do you currently teach most often?"
- Options:
  * Preschool (Ages 2-5)
  * Children (Grades 1-5)
  * Youth (Grades 6-12)
  * College/Young Adult
  * Adult

**Question 6: Theology Profile**
- Type: Multiple choice
- Required: Yes
- Label: "Which theological perspective best describes your church?"
- Options:
  * Southern Baptist Convention (SBC)
  * Cooperative Baptist Fellowship (CBF)
  * Moderate Baptist
  * Conservative Baptist

**Question 7: Tech Comfort Level**
- Type: Multiple choice
- Required: Yes
- Label: "How comfortable are you with technology?"
- Options:
  * Very comfortable - I'm tech-savvy
  * Comfortable - I use apps regularly
  * Somewhat comfortable - I can figure things out
  * Not very comfortable - I prefer simple tools

**Question 8: Referral** (Optional)
- Type: Short answer
- Required: No
- Label: "Who invited you to beta test? (Optional)"

**Question 9: Availability**
- Type: Checkboxes
- Required: Yes
- Label: "I understand and agree to:"
- Options (must check all):
  * Generate 3-5 lessons over the next 3-4 weeks
  * Provide honest feedback
  * Join the private Facebook group
  * Participate in one follow-up conversation

## Step 3: Configure Settings
1. Click "Settings" (gear icon at top)
2. General tab:
   - CHECK: "Limit to 1 response"
   - CHECK: "Collect email addresses"
3. Presentation tab:
   - Confirmation message: "Thank you! You'll receive an email within 24 hours with your login credentials and Facebook group invitation."

## Step 4: Get the Link
1. Click "Send" button (top right)
2. Click the link icon (chain link symbol)
3. Click "Shorten URL"
4. Copy the shortened link (looks like: https://forms.gle/abc123xyz)

## Step 5: Set Up Response Notifications
1. Click the "Responses" tab
2. Click the three dots menu (•••)
3. Select "Get email notifications for new responses"

## Step 6: Create Response Spreadsheet
1. In "Responses" tab, click green Sheets icon
2. Select "Create a new spreadsheet"
3. Name it: "BibleBibleLessonSpark Beta Sign-Ups"
4. This will auto-populate with all submissions

## Step 7: Update Your Email Template
1. Open: C:\Users\Lynn\lesson-spark-usa\BETA_INVITATION_EMAIL.txt
2. Replace [YOUR-GOOGLE-FORM-ID] with your actual form link
3. Save the file

DONE! You now have a professional beta sign-up form.
